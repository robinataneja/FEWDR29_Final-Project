Shipping Policy

Once you have placed your order & it has been confirmed,
please allow 7-10 business days for standard shipping
& 3-4 business days for express shipping.

Due to Covid-19 delivery times may be delayed.

The cost of standard shipping for orders under
$100 is $9.95 & for orders over $100 is free.

The cost of express shipping is $20.

Please note that once your order has been dispatched,
unfortunately delays with our courier are out of our hands & beyond our control.
